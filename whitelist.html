How to find your MTA Serial

  1. Open Multi Theft Auto (MTA)
  2. Join any server (or singleplayer)
  3. Open chat by pressing T
  4. Type /serial and press Enter
  5. Copy the serial shown (it will look like: 5A3F8B2C1D9E7F6A0B4C8D2E1F3A5B7C)
  6. Paste it in the form above

Note: Your serial is unique to your MTA installation and helps us prevent ban evasion.
